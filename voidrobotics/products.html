Void Products and Services

Void's world-class products can solve a multitude of your production and creation needs. Here is what we have to offer.
CycleMotor™ and CycleMotor™ Pro
The CycleMotor™ is a powerful, lightweight DC motor with a variety of use cases from driving wheels to arms to heavy machinery. The CycleMotor™ Pro has twice the torque for twice the strength, and comes with an additional 2-month warranty.
 
AngleServo™ and AngleServo™ SE
For smaller projects with lower budgets, AngleServo™ SE fits in an even smaller package while maintaining precision and function. Both AngleServo™ and AngleServo™ SE are compatible with the Void® library of servo horn attachments, making connecting AngleServo™ to anything a breeze.
Pivoting has never been easier than with AngleServo™. This ultra-precise, blockchain-controlled servo motor has the strengh yet tiny size you need. AngleServo™ uses ultrafine gears to produce precision down to 1/1000th of a degree, unparalleled in the industry.
MecanumTerrain™ wheels and TerrainKit™ components
These rugged wheels can traverse any type of ground, roll smoothly over bumps and grooves, and move in all directions. Void® MecanumTerrain™ wheels are reinforced with titanium so they sstay strong and can defeat the terrain beneath them.
TerrainKit™, an optional add-on to MecanumTerrain™, is a catalog of components that ensure your wheel is being used to its fullest potential. From precise-length axles to custom-holed brackets and bearing sockets, TerrainKit™ is a must-have for any new MecanumTerrain™ owner.
